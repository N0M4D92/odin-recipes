French Bread Pizza

Description

Save time and the money in your wallet by making a pizza with fresh, crispy French bread. Kids and adults alike will enjoy the variety and simplicity of this classic dish. Bake in an oven, air-fryer or microwave.

Ingredients

  • French bread
  • tomato sauce
  • garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • butter
  • oregano
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • toppings (sausage, veggies, etc.)

    • Directions

    1. Cut bread in half lengthwise, brushing it with garlic butter.
    2. Top bread halves with tomato sauce, sprinkle on oregano, cheeses, and toppings of choice.
    3. Place on a baking sheet and bake until browned.
