Mango Ice Cream

Description

Enjoy this simple frozen treat any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • Heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup fresh mango
  • Raw honey (to taste)

    • Directions

    1. Puree the mango. Take care to remove the skin. Taste the pulp, adding honey accordingly.
    2. Mix the mango puree and the whipping cream into a bowl. Whip one to two minutes.
    3. Pour the combined mixture into a container with a lid. Freeze the ice cream for 5-6 hours or until set.
    4. When ready to eat, let the ice cream thaw for five minutes to soften a little. Scoop and serve!
